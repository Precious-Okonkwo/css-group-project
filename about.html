Our Story

Where Every Meal Feels Like Home

Our Story

Inspired by the charm of Italian streets and the warmth of shared meals, Cipasso was created as a space where food brings people together. From the first aroma to the last bite, every moment is designed to feel like home.

Rooted in tradition and guided by passion, our kitchen celebrates authentic Italian recipes passed down through generations. Each dish is prepared with fresh ingredients, simple techniques, and a deep love for flavour.

Whether it’s a quiet dinner, a lively gathering, or a spontaneous stop for wine, Cipasso invites you to slow down, savour, and enjoy life the Italian way.

Our Philosophy

We believe great food doesn’t need to be complicated. At Cipasso, it’s about honest ingredients, bold flavours, and the joy of sharing a table. Good wine, good company, and unforgettable moments — that’s our promise.

Meet Our Chef

Chef Marco Rossi brings a deep love for traditional Italian cuisine, shaped by years of experience and a passion for simplicity done right. His approach focuses on letting ingredients speak for themselves.

From handmade pasta to carefully curated wine pairings, every dish reflects his commitment to authenticity, warmth, and excellence.
